### ğŸ”¹ Lab 5: Persistent Volumes & Claims

**PersistentVolume (PV):**

```yaml

metadata:
  name: pv-demo
spec:
  capacity:
    storage: 5Gi
  accessModes:
    - ReadWriteOnce
  hostPath:
    path: "/mnt/data"
```

**PersistentVolumeClaim (PVC):**

```yaml

kind: PersistentVolumeClaim
metadata:
  name: pvc-demo
spec:
  accessModes:
    - ReadWriteOnce
  resources:
    requests:
      storage: 5Gi
```

Mount in pod:

```yaml

- mountPath: "/data"
  name: pvc-demo
```
